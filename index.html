Welcome to Our Pack!

We are Cub Scout Pack 242, based in Newport News, VA. Our pack is dedicated to providing a fun and educational program for boys and girls that builds character, trains them in the responsibilities of citizenship, and develops personal fitness. Join us for exciting adventures!
