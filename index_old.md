# Welcome to Cub Scout Pack 242! 

Cub Scout Pack 242 welcomes boys & girls ages Kindergarten through 5th grade. We meet Monday nights at 6:30pm at St. Andrew's Episcopal Church, 45 Main St Newport News, VA.
